About West Wayanad Spices

At West Wayanad Spices, we bring you the finest natural spices grown in the fertile valleys and forested hills of Wayanad, Kerala. We are committed to offering authentic flavors while preserving the traditional essence of our spice heritage.

  • 100% Organic & Chemical-Free
  • Sourced Directly from Local Farmers
  • Hygienically Cleaned & Sun-Dried
  • Packed with Aroma & Traditional Taste

Our range includes turmeric, pepper, ginger, tamarind, clove, and many more — all packed with love and care. Every grain tells the story of Kerala’s rich biodiversity and farming culture.
